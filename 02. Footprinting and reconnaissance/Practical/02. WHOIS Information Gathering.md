## 📌 Overview

WHOIS is a query protocol used to retrieve domain registration details from publicly available databases. It is an essential technique in the reconnaissance phase of ethical hacking.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify domain ownership details  
- Retrieve registration and expiration information  
- Discover registrar and DNS servers  
- Gather publicly available administrative data
