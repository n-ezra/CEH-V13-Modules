# 📌 What is Hacking?

### Hacking is the process of identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in computer systems, networks, or applications to gain unauthorized access or control.

### It can be performed for both malicious and ethical (security testing) purposes.

## 🎯 Purpose of Hacking
### To test system security (ethical hacking)
### To identify vulnerabilities
### To protect systems from cyber attacks
### To gain unauthorized access (malicious intent)

## 🧑‍💻 Types of Hackers

### Hackers are individuals who use their technical skills to identify vulnerabilities in systems, networks, or applications. Based on their intent and activities, hackers are classified into different categories.

## 🟢 White Hat Hackers (Ethical Hackers)
### Authorized professionals who perform security testing
### Work legally to identify and fix vulnerabilities
### Help organizations improve cybersecurity
### Also known as Ethical Hackers / Penetration Testers
## 🔴 Black Hat Hackers
### Malicious hackers with illegal intentions
### Exploit vulnerabilities for personal gain
### Activities include:
### Data theft
### Ransomware attacks
### Unauthorized access
## ⚪ Gray Hat Hackers
### Combination of white and black hat hackers
### May break rules but usually not for malicious purposes
### Sometimes report vulnerabilities after exploiting them
## 🟡 Script Kiddies
### Beginners with limited knowledge
### Use pre-built tools and scripts
### Do not create their own exploits
## 🔵 Hacktivists
### Hackers driven by political or social causes
### Target organizations or governments
### Aim to spread awareness or protest
## 🟣 State-Sponsored Hackers
### Work for governments or intelligence agencies
### Conduct cyber espionage and cyber warfare
### Target other countries or organizations
## 🟠 Cyber Terrorists
### Use hacking to create fear and disruption
### Target critical infrastructure (power, banking, etc.)
### Motivated by ideology
## ⚠️ Legal Note

### Only White Hat Hacking (Ethical Hacking) is legal when performed with proper authorization. All other malicious activities are illegal and punishable by law.
